The Welcoming Woods

As I walk in the forest, the birds are chirping and welcoming. It's a beautiful sunny spring day, with fluffy clouds in the sky. Gentle is the breeze that passes through my hair calmly.

Around me are strong thick trees that look as if they can never be moved; firmly rooted deep in the ground. As I look up, green fresh leaves hover over me like a canopy. I notice gentle movements at the tips of the trees as they sway in the wind; as if they are dancing. Small squirrels are jumping, and chasing one another around in the entwined branches, talking to one another.
